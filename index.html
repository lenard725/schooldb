Welcome to Sta. Juliana High School in Capas, Tarlac. We're a public school serving students from Grade 7 to Senior High School. Our goal is to provide you with a great education and help you grow. We believe that education is essential. We have teachers and programs that will help you learn and become a better person. On this website, you can learn more about our school, what we offer, and how to be a part of our community. Education opens doors to a brighter future, and we're here to help you every step of the way. Welcome to Sta. Juliana High School, where learning is exciting, and your potential is unlimited. Join us on this journey towards success.
